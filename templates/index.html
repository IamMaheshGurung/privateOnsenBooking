Welcome to Kwangdi Pahuna Ghar

Nestled in the serene Shantipur valley of Gulmi district, our traditional Nepali guesthouse offers an authentic experience with modern comforts. Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of rural Nepal while enjoying warm hospitality and delicious local cuisine.

At Kwangdi Pahuna Ghar, we strive to provide a peaceful retreat where you can escape city life and connect with Nepal's rich cultural heritage and natural splendor.
