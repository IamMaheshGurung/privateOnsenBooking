Welcome to Our Traditional Ryokan

Nestled in the serene mountains, Kwangdi Hotel offers an authentic Japanese ryokan experience. Our carefully preserved traditional architecture and gardens create a peaceful atmosphere that allows guests to connect with nature and Japanese culture.

Every room features traditional tatami flooring, sliding paper doors, and minimalist aesthetics that embody the Japanese principles of simplicity and harmony with nature.

Private Onsen Experience

Immerse yourself in the healing waters of our private onsen baths. Each onsen is fed by natural hot springs and surrounded by carefully arranged rocks and plants to create a serene atmosphere.

Our private onsens can be booked exclusively for your personal use, allowing you to enjoy the traditional Japanese bathing experience in complete privacy and tranquility.
